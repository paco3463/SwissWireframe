Our Story

A Delco Original Since 1968

For over five decades, we've been the fastest friendly stop in the county — a drive-thru market built on convenience, quality, and knowing our neighbors by name.

How It Started

Milk Without Leaving the Car

It began with a simple idea: what if you could grab the essentials without unbuckling the kids? That drive-thru dairy stand grew into a Delco institution.

Today we're refreshing the experience for a new generation — same speed, same warmth, sharper than ever.

The Journey

Five Decades, One Window

  • 1968 — The First Stand

    A single drive-thru dairy opens its window in Delaware County.

  • 1980s — Growing the Menu

    Hoagies, fresh bread, and grab-and-go essentials join the lineup.

  • 2000s — A County Habit

    Multiple neighborhood stores make Swiss Farms part of the daily routine.

  • Today — Reinvented

    A refreshed brand, modern stores, and the same fast, friendly service.
